Privacy Policy for Happy Kids

1. Introduction

Welcome to Happy Kids! Your child's privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect any personal information while using our mobile app.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from children.

However, we may collect non-personal data such as:

  • App usage statistics
  • Device type and operating system
  • Crash reports for improving app performance

3. How We Use Information

We use non-personal data to improve app performance and user experience.

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

4. Advertisements & Third-Party Services

Our app may display advertisements from Google AdMob.

Google may use the collected data to provide personalized ads. However, our app complies with the Google Play Families Policy, ensuring ads are child-appropriate.

5. Parental Controls

We encourage parents to supervise their child's use of our app. If you have any concerns, please contact us.

6. Data Security

We take security seriously and ensure that your child's data remains protected.

7. Your Rights

As a parent, you have the right to:

  • Request access to any data collected
  • Delete any information (if applicable)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app.
